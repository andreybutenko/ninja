How can I solve problems?

Understanding

The only way to genuinely address a problem is to understand the person you're designing for and their unique challenges - empathy. In all my recent projects, I've interviewed the people I'm designing for to know what needs I'm trying to satisfy.

Code

My specialty is the web. I develop for the full-stack: front-end, back-end, and databases. This lets me understand not just how to achieve a technical feature on one level, but how to integrate that functionality in the most efficient, clean, and maintainable way.

Knowledge

I believe understanding through multiple perspectives leads to the most effective, most creative solutions. While I'm in school, I'm studying as many of these perspectives as I can. I study music, business, design, engineering, and others. Understanding these different areas of knowledge allows me to produce better results.
